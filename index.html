POISE is a flagship initiative organized by AIESEC in CINEC, bringing together youth, fashion professionals, and personal branding experts into one vibrant and transformative learning environment. This experience celebrates confidence, professional poise, and self-expression across diverse domains.

More than just a workshop, POISE provides a launchpad for young leaders to build high-stakes project management skills, master corporate negotiations, and experience real-world execution. Through strategic planning, hands-on team leadership, and event execution, POISE creates a space where potential turns into performance.
